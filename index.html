Prediction Demo: 30-Day Readmission Risk ?

What is being predicted? ? For each diabetes inpatient encounter, the model estimates whether the patient may be readmitted to the hospital within 30 days after discharge. This is a portfolio baseline model, not a clinical decision tool.

Where the prediction appears in this dashboard

  • Target: readmitted within 30 days, shown as the prediction outcome.
  • Model metrics: AUC, precision, recall, and F1 summarize prediction performance.
  • Model signals: prior inpatient visits, number of diagnoses, emergency visits, age, and length of stay are examples of features that move risk up or down.

Project Value

This project demonstrates the full healthcare analytics workflow: data profiling, KPI design, cohort segmentation, dashboard reporting, data quality review, and a predictive modeling baseline.

It is designed for Healthcare Data Analyst, Clinical BI Analyst, Quality Reporting Analyst, and entry-level Population Health Analytics applications.
